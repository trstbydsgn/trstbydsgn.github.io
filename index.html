About Me

I help medical device organizations design, deploy, and scale secure, compliant, and clinically reliable products and infrastructure.


I focus on embedding trust into the product lifecycle—from secure-by-design engineering and AI model governance to data integrity, post-market monitoring, and regulatory alignment.


I work to ensure innovation accelerates without compromising patient safety, clinical confidence, or regulatory predictability.


In parallel, I serve as an angel investor and advisor, supporting early-stage companies building infrastructure, platforms, and tools at the intersection of AI, security, and trust in regulated industries.


My focus is simple:
turn trust into a competitive advantage.
