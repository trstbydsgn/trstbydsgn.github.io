About Me

I lead at the intersection of Product Cybersecurity, AI Security & Governance, and Trust & Compliance. I help medical device organizations design, deploy, and scale secure, compliant, and clinically reliable AI-enabled products and infrastructure. With deep experience in regulated, safety-critical environments, I focus on embedding trust into the product lifecycle—from secure-by-design engineering and AI model governance to data integrity, post-market monitoring, and regulatory alignment. As medical devices become increasingly software- and AI-driven, I work to ensure innovation accelerates without compromising patient safety, clinical confidence, or regulatory predictability. I currently lead a large cross-functional product security organization, partnering closely with R&D, Quality, Regulatory, Clinical, and executive leadership to modernize assurance models for the next generation of digital health technology. In parallel, I serve as an angel investor and advisor, supporting early-stage companies building infrastructure, platforms, and tools at the intersection of AI, security, and trust in regulated industries. My focus is simple: turn trust into a competitive advantage.
